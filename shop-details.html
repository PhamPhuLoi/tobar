Cocktail (long drink) is a mixed drink containing alcohol. A cocktail usually includes alcohol and flavors such as liqueurs, fruit, honey, milk or cream and other additives. According to the American newspaper Balance, in 1806, a cocktail was defined as a drink made from various spirits, sugar, water, and "bitters" and this definition was used throughout the century. 19. By the 20th century, cocktails were transformed differently from the original. Nearly 200 years later, May 13, 1806 was taken as the anniversary date when cocktails appeared in newspapers. Regarding the name cocktail, if translated correctly in English, cocktail means rooster tail. Accordingly, at the time of America's founding, cockfighting was one of the very popular games. The winning chicken owner will receive the losing chicken's tail feathers and be treated to a round of wine after being toasted "on the cocktail". After that, people used this name to refer to a drink mixed from many ingredients (ie cocktail) because its color is as colorful and colorful as a rooster's tail. According to some other documents, the name cocktail comes from a Frenchman named Antoine Peychaud. He mixed a drink from whiskey and some other ingredients, naming it coquetier. After many years, the word coquetier was spelled cocktail.

Going back in history to the years 1749 - 1756, Kleriker Israel Acrelius, a Swede, while visiting the British colony, reported on more than 45 types of drinks mixed with ingredients such as wine, lemon, and sweet vinegar. Since 1800, Saloons (similar to Bars) have sprung up to serve the entertainment needs of cowboys, and whiskey has also become more popular. However, American whiskey at that time was very heavy and not aged, so the taste was quite bad. People must add a little sugar, honey and flavoring ingredients or fruits to make it easier to drink. This mixed drink is gradually loved by many people. This is also the foundation of the birth of cocktails. So it can be said that cocktails appeared earliest in America By the mid-19th century, there were many other better wines produced in America. In the early years of the 20th century, the United States enacted laws banning alcohol and cocktails became the main drink thanks to their low alcohol content. After World War II, following the expeditions of American soldiers, cocktails appeared popular in Europe. One of the oldest recognized cocktail recipes is the Martini.
